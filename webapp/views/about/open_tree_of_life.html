Open Tree of Life is an NSF funded project that aims to construt a comprehensive, dynamic and digitally-available tree of life by synthesizing published phylogenetic trees along with taxonomic data.

The project is a collaborative effort between 11 PIs across 10 institutions. See the Credits page for more information about collaborators and contributors.

Funding for OpenTree comes from NSF AVAToL #1208809
